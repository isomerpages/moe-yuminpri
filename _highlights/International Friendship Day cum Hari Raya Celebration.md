Our school came alive on 9 April 2026 as we marked International Friendship Day cum Hari Raya Celebration. Students explored Singapore’s role in an interconnected world while deepening their understanding of ASEAN, with a focus on the Philippines as the 2026 Chair. The celebration also highlighted shared values such as respect, hospitality and community spirit.

Through a range of engaging activities, including a Hari Raya sing-along, performances, skits and interactive recess stations, students learnt more about different cultures and traditions. The event fostered cultural understanding, strengthened friendships and reinforced the importance of regional connections.
