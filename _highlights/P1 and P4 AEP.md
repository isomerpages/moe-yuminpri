**Arts Education Programme (AEP)**

The Arts Education Programme (AEP) is an interdisciplinary programme that integrates the English, Music and Physical Education (PE) curricula to develop students holistically. Through authentic and engaging learning experiences, students develop their creativity, communication skills and collaborative competencies. Over 8 to 12 lessons within each discipline, students build their communication, singing and dance skills before culminating in a showcase where they perform and share their learning.

**P1 Festival of the Arts**

The P1 Festival of the Arts, held on 20 May 2026, showcased the talents of our Primary 1 students through a lively series of performances and presentations. Developed through a 10-week Speech and Drama programme as well as music and dance programmes, our students confidently presented Choral Reading, Readers’ Theatre, Poetry Recitation, and song and dance items. The event also saw strong support from parents, creating a warm atmosphere of encouragement and pride.

**P4 AEP Showcase**

On 28 May 2026, our Primary 4 students took centre stage as young broadcast journalists, bringing stories from around the world to life through drama, music and dance. Through confident performances, students showcased stories that reflected our school values of Care, Courage, Integrity, Resilience, Respect and Responsibility. The vibrant showcase highlighted the interdisciplinary skills developed through the integration of English, Music and PE lessons as part of the school’s Arts Education Programme.
