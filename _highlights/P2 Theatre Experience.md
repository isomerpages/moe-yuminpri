<p>As part of our ongoing efforts to nurture students who can engage thoughtfully
with stories and express their ideas with confidence, our Primary 2 students
attended Singapore Repertory Theatre's production of Charlotte's Web at
the Drama Centre Theatre in the National Library Building. The performance
explored themes of friendship, loyalty, and loss through the story of Wilbur
the pig and Charlotte the spider. Filled with humour and lively performances,
the show held our students' attention and brought these themes to life
in a memorable way.</p>
