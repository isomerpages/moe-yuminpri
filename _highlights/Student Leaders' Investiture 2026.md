On 2 April 2026, our school community gathered in the newly air-conditioned hall for the Student Leaders' Investiture, which was also livestreamed to classrooms across the school. Led by Head Prefect Nur Wafiyya Zhou Yuxin Binte Muhammad Yazid, our student leaders took their pledge, affirming their commitment to lead with care and serve with P.R.I.D.E. These student leaders were selected for their demonstrated ability to communicate with confidence, work collaboratively, and show care for those around them. The ceremony concluded on a heartwarming note as student leaders expressed their gratitude to their parents and invited them to share in celebrating this meaningful milestone together.
