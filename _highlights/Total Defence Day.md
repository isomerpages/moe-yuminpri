<h4>Total Defence Day</h4>
<p>Total Defence Day on 15 February reminded us that safeguarding Singapore
is a shared responsibility. At Yumin, students engaged in activities that
highlighted how their everyday actions contribute to Total Defence. The
event fostered resilience, unity, and responsibility in protecting our
nation's future whilst reinforcing the importance of standing together
as one people.</p>
