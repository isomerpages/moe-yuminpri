<p>Champions' Day officially launched Yumin Primary School's 50th Anniversary
celebrations, bringing together our entire school community—staff, students,
and parents—in joyful commemoration of this milestone year.</p>
<p>The highlight of the day was the Primary 3 Arts Education Programme (AEP)
Showcase, where students transformed Yumin's rich history into captivating
performances. From scenes inspired by the school's early days to vibrant
creative expressions, the showcase demonstrated how our young learners
had engaged deeply with Yumin's remarkable story and heritage.</p>
<p>The celebration featured prize-giving ceremonies and delightful performances,
creating a joyful occasion that honoured both achievement and effort. Together,
students, staff, and guests celebrated not only what Yumin has accomplished
over five decades, but also what it continues to evolve into as a vibrant
learning community.</p>
<p>This memorable launch set the tone for our year-long golden jubilee celebrations,
marking the beginning of a special journey through Yumin's past, present,
and future.</p>
