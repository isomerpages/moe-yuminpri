<p><em>Celebrating Our Past, Inspiring the Future</em>
</p>
<p>2025 marked the beginning of Yumin Primary School's golden jubilee celebrations,
a momentous milestone spanning 2025 and 2026. Under our commemorative theme
"Celebrating Our Past, Inspiring the Future", our school community came
together to honour our rich heritage, celebrate our people, and reaffirm
our unwavering commitment to nurturing passionate, future-ready learners
and gracious citizens.</p>
<p>Throughout this special year, students, staff, and partners reflected
on Yumin's remarkable journey—from our founding in 1975 to the vibrant
learning community we had become. These celebrations honoured our proud
legacy whilst propelling us forward with renewed purpose and unity.</p>
