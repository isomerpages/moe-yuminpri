<p>To kick off our 50th anniversary year, Teachers' Day 2025 transported
the school back to the decade when Yumin was founded. With the theme "Turn
the Beat Around", staff embraced 1970s-inspired outfits, filling the school
with retro charm, vibrant colours, and warm nostalgia.</p>
<p>Through laughter, music, and shared memories, the celebration served as
both a light-hearted tribute to the pioneers who laid Yumin Primary School's
foundations and a gentle reminder that whilst times change, the essence
of teaching remains constant—caring deeply for every child and nurturing
their growth with dedication and love.</p>
