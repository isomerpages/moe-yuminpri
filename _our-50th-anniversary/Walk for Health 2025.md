<p>As part of our 50th Anniversary celebrations, all Yumin Champions and
staff participated in the Walk for Health, transforming Children's Day
into a meaningful act of giving back to the community. Our pupils ventured
into the surrounding neighbourhood, united by a shared purpose of helping
others. Through our partnership with South East CDC and FairPrice Foundation,
every kilometre walked was matched with one dollar worth of supermarket
vouchers to support families in need.</p>
<p>This collective effort raised $1,792 worth of supermarket vouchers.The
Walk for Health embodied Yumin's commitment to nurturing gracious citizens
who actively care for their community. Every step taken served as a reminder
that even small actions, when done together, can make a real difference
in the lives of others</p>
