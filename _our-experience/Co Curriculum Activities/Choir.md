<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To cultivate the joy of singing and acquire vocal skills and techniques
in singing in both solfege and parts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To promote appreciation of the various choral works and cultural music.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To work collaboratively to apply their learning and showcase their skills.</p>

</li>
</ul>
