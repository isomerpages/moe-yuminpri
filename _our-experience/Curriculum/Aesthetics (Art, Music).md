<p>Arts is integral to the holistic development of our pupils. We believe
that immersing our students in arts experiences will enhance students'
life and their wellbeing as well as deepening their appreciation of local
and global culture.</p>
<p></p>
<h4>Framework or Pedagogical Approaches:</h4>
<p>In Yumin, we believe there is arts talent in every student waiting to
be discovered. Every student will be given equal opportunities to be developed
in the Arts regardless of their background. With the students' arts engagement
through the Type 1 activities and arts enrichment through the Type II activities
of their choice and interest, we hope to provide our students with a myriad
arts activities across the levels for them to discover their interest and
develop their arts knowledge and skills. This will enable students with
potential and interest to excel in the arts forms. Hopefully, they will
apply the learnt arts skills and contribute back to the community, hence
bringing joy and meaning to enrich the lives of others.</p>
