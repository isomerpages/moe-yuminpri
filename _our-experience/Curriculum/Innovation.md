<p><strong>Innovation @ Yumin Primary School</strong>
</p>
<p>At Yumin Primary School, innovation is nurtured through a school-wide
approach that integrates sustainability, STEAM learning and 21st Century
Competencies (21CC). Students are guided to develop curiosity, creativity
and problem-solving skills through authentic learning experiences. Students
are encouraged to explore ideas, design solutions and take action to care
for the environment and community.</p>

<p><strong>Applied Learning Programme (ALP)</strong>
</p>
<p>Yumin Primary School’s <strong>Applied Learning Programme (ALP)</strong> is
anchored on the theme of <strong>Sustainability</strong>.</p>
<p>Our Applied Learning Programme (ALP) empowers students to explore real-world
sustainability challenges through <strong>Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics (STEAM)</strong>.</p>
<p>The programme provides a platform for <strong>Yumin Champions</strong> to
develop emerging <strong>21st Century Competencies</strong> through meaningful,
hands-on learning experiences.</p>
<p><strong>ALP Signature Thinking Model – APP-IT</strong>
</p>
<p>To support students in developing structured thinking and innovation skills,
Yumin has adapted the <strong>Engineering Thinking Process</strong> into
a school-based model known as <strong>APP-IT!</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2__2026_APPIT_.png">

<p>From Type 1 to Type 3, students utilize <strong>APP-IT! Thinking Process</strong> to
understand the problem presented to them and do more research.</p>

<p><strong>Catalyst@Yumin</strong>
</p>
<p>Catalyst@Yumin is Yumin Primary School’s signature Applied Learning Programme
focused on sustainability.</p>
<p>Through tinkering, coding and design projects, students explore environmental
issues and develop innovative solutions that benefit the community and
the environment.</p>
<p>The programme encourages students to:</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Think
critically about real-world problems</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Design
creative solutions</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Work
collaboratively</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Develop
a strong sense of environmental stewardship</p>
<p>By nurturing curiosity and innovation, Catalyst@Yumin empowers students
to become responsible changemakers for a sustainable future.</p>
