P6 students can seek admission to certain secondary schools based on their talent in sports, CCAs and specific academic areas. The Direct School Admission application for Secondary Schools (DSA-Sec) is currently closed.

  

Please click [here](https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/dsa) for more information.
