<h4>Total Defence Day</h4>
<p>Total Defence Day on 15 February reminded us that safeguarding Singapore
is a shared responsibility. At Yumin, students engaged in activities that
highlighted how their everyday actions contribute to Total Defence. The
event fostered resilience, unity, and responsibility in protecting our
nation's future whilst reinforcing the importance of standing together
as one people.</p>

<h4>Chinese New Year Celebration</h4>
<p>The spirit of Chinese New Year came alive at Yumin as students explored
Chinese culture through engaging, hands-on experiences beyond the classroom.</p>
<p>Celebrating the Year of the Horse alongside Yumin's 50th anniversary and
10 years of Positive Education, our Yumin Champions designed decorative
horses echoing our theme "Never Stop to Grow" (马不停蹄，永不放弃).</p>
<p>The celebration culminated in an exciting level-based competition, filling
the school with joy and festive cheer whilst strengthening character and
uniting the Yumin community in celebrating both tradition and our significant
milestones.</p>

<h4>PSG Get-Together Session</h4>
<p>In Term 1, Yumin's Parent Support Group (PSG) members came together to
strengthen the vital partnership between home and school—an essential foundation
for supporting every child's learning and well-being. The session facilitated
meaningful dialogue and sharing on resources that can support parent partnership
and involvement in their children's education.</p>
<p>This collaborative approach reinforces our belief that when parents and
school work together, students thrive both academically and personally.</p>

<h4>P5 Camp Resilience</h4>
<p>From 28 to 30 January 2026, our Primary 5 students participated in a 3-day,
2-night Camp Resilience at Sarimbun Scout Camp, stepping boldly out of
their comfort zones.</p>
<p>Through outdoor challenges, team-building activities, and a memorable
campfire session, students built confidence, strengthened friendships,
and developed valuable lessons in resilience and teamwork that will serve
them well beyond the camp experience.</p>
