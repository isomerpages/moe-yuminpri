<h4>P5 Camp Resilience</h4>
<p>From 28 to 30 January 2026, our Primary 5 students participated in a 3-day,
2-night Camp Resilience at Sarimbun Scout Camp, stepping boldly out of
their comfort zones.</p>
<p>Through outdoor challenges, team-building activities, and a memorable
campfire session, students built confidence, strengthened friendships,
and developed valuable lessons in resilience and teamwork that will serve
them well beyond the camp experience.</p>
